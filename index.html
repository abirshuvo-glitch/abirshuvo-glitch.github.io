01 — About

Who I am and how I approach security

Security plus startups. I care about systems that work when people are tired, stressed, and on cheap hardware.

I’m a Cybersecurity-focused IT student with a foundation in Python, SQL, HTML/CSS, and networking. Most of my work is around understanding threats, documenting risk, and designing flows that a real team can follow at 2 AM without guessing.

I’m also the founder of DokanBondhu, a smart ledger & sales platform for Bangladeshi “mudi dokan”. That forces me to think about reality: low-end phones, shared devices, slow networks, and users who don’t care about “cyber” — they just want their money tracked.

Right now I’m doubling down on CCNA networking labs, OWASP Top 10, and BCP / incident response, while building projects that look like actual work for a junior SOC analyst or security engineer.
